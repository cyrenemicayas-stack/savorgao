Siargao Food Crawl Itinerary

Day 1: General Luna – A Day of Beachside Dining & Sunset Views

Morning:

Mama’s Grill – A local favorite for hearty breakfast or brunch. Enjoy grilled meats and home-cooked Filipino classics. This is a beloved Filipino restaurant chain that has captured the hearts of locals and tourists alike, especially in Siargao. Known for its delicious and affordable grilled dishes, the restaurant offers a cozy, open-air dining experience perfect for families and groups. Some of their standout offerings include Inihaw na Liempo (grilled pork belly), Inihaw na Baboy (grilled pork), Inihaw na Manok (grilled chicken), and sizzling Sisig, complemented by refreshing Halo-Halo for dessert. The relaxed atmosphere, often accompanied by live acoustic music, makes Mama's Grill a favorite spot for an enjoyable meal in a tropical setting. Whether you're a fan of barbecue or looking for authentic Filipino flavors, Mama's Grill provides a satisfying and memorable dining experience.

Midday:

Shaka Siargao – Fresh smoothie bowls and wraps with a view of Cloud 9. is a vibrant beachfront café famous for its fresh, healthy offerings and relaxed island vibe. Located near the iconic Cloud 9 surf spot, it’s a favorite among travelers and surfers looking for nourishing meals after a day in the sun. The menu features colorful smoothie bowls, fresh fruit juices, hearty wraps, and vegan-friendly options—all served with a view of the ocean. With its eco-conscious ethos and tropical atmosphere, Shaka perfectly captures the laid-back, health-conscious spirit of Siargao.

Afternoon:

Bravo Restaurant – Enjoy seafood paella and tapas at the beachside. It nestled within Bravo Beach Resort in General Luna, Siargao, offers a delightful fusion of Spanish and Asian cuisines, creating a unique dining experience that reflects the island's diverse culinary influences. Guests can savor dishes like the Slow Cooked Pork Curry, enriched with coconut sambal, and the Tuna Tataki, a pan-seared tuna delicacy infused with Japanese flair. The restaurant's beachfront location provides picturesque views of the Pesangan Reef and the Pacific Ocean, enhancing the dining ambiance. With its thatched roof and open-air design, Bravo Restaurant exudes a laid-back, tropical vibe, making it an ideal spot for both intimate meals and lively gatherings.

Sibol Siargao – is a vibrant and popular café located on the beautiful island of Siargao. It is known for its cozy, laid-back ambiance and its focus on serving fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.
